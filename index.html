Hair

Discover trichologist developed solutions that help address your hair fall concerns and support further hair growth.

What to expect?

1-2 months

There will likely be no visible change in the first two months. Some people may shed slightly more hair strands than before. This is temporary and absolutely normal.

3-6 months

At this point, it may become noticeable that hair loss has slowed, stopped, or in some cases maybe even reversed. Take a photo to track your progress.

After 6+ months

By now, hair loss may have considerably slowed down or stopped, and some people may see signs of regrowth (usually at the crown of the head). Consistent daily treatment is required to maintain results.

PCOS

Restore hormonal imbalance and manage bloating, period cramps, stress and more with our period management range.

What to expect?

3-6 months

At this point, it may become noticeable that your stress and anxiety levels have reduced. Some people may notice an improvement in energy level & digestion. Your body may feel lighter; you’re able to manage your weight better.

After 6+ months

By now, your hormonal imbalance may have reduced. Your periods are more regular than before. Consistent daily treatment is required to maintain results and manage PCOS symptoms and period cramps .

Feminine Care

Manage intimate hygiene, reduce UTI recurrence and improve gut health with our intimate care product range.

What to expect?

3-6 months

At this point, it may become noticeable, the recurrence of UTI is reduced. The UTI causing bacteria is prevented from sticking to the walls of the urinary tract reducing the likelihood of infection. The intimate area skin is moisturized and limits further chaffing, excessive sweating reducing the likelihood of bacterial infections.

After 6+ months

By now, you may notice an improvement in gut health. This aids the growth of good bacteria & helps prevent UTIs by keeping bad bacteria from growing in the urinary tract.
